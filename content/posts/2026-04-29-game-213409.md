Get ready, mobile gamers! HAOPLAY has just unveiled their latest creation, Quack Quack Attack - PEGG Blaster, a casual puzzle game that promises to redefine the genre. Set to launch on iOS and Android later this year, players will embark on an oceanic adventure as Captain Quack, battling monsters and overcoming obstacles with the help of a diverse squad of birds. The games unique pachinko-style mechanics and strategic bird selection are sure to captivate both casual and hardcore gamers alike.

What sets Quack Quack Attack apart is its depth of gameplay. With up to fifty unique birds across six classes, players can experiment with different combinations to devise the perfect strategy for each level. The ability to evolve birds, customize abilities, and forge new equipment adds layers of complexity that will keep players engaged long after the initial launch. Plus, with multiple game modes like the Purgatory Tower and Starry Expedition, theres something for everyone.

Pre-registration is now open, offering exciting rewards for early sign-ups. As we await the official launch, its clear that Quack Quack Attack - PEGG Blaster is poised to make a significant impact in the mobile gaming landscape. Will you join Captain Quack on this epic journey?
