In an exciting twist for mobile gamers, two iconic video game franchises, Tetris and Space Invaders, have united for their first-ever collaboration. This partnership brings a fresh mode to Tetris, allowing players to engage in thrilling events that blend the classic puzzle gameplay with the nostalgic alien invasion theme of Space Invaders. Starting today, players can dive into three unique events that promise to enhance their gaming experience.

The collaboration kicks off with the Days of Invaders event, running from April 1-12, where players can log in daily to collect stickers and unlock exclusive rewards. Following this, the Earth Protection Events, in partnership with Dots.eco, will run from April 13-23, allowing players to contribute to wildlife conservation through their gameplay. Finally, the excitement culminates in the Invaders Showdown Tournament from April 24-30, where players can compete for top scores and exclusive prizes.

This innovative crossover not only celebrates the legacy of both games but also demonstrates how gaming can contribute to real-world causes. As we look forward to more collaborations like this, one has to wonder: what other classic games could benefit from a modern twist?
