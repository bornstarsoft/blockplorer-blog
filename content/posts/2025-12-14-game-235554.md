The excitement surrounding the upcoming game, Star Wars: Fate of the Old Republic, is palpable, especially with director Casey Hudson's recent comments dispelling rumors of a lengthy development timeline. Hudson, known for his pivotal roles in the original Knights of the Old Republic and the Mass Effect series, emphasized on social media that fans wont have to wait until 2030 for the games release, stating, "Im not getting any younger!"

Fate of the Old Republic is being developed by Hudsons new studio, Arcanaut, and is described as a single-player, narrative-driven action RPG. This project marks a significant return to the Star Wars universe for Hudson, who co-founded Arcanaut with the aim of creating emotionally impactful and immersive gaming experiences. The game is still in its early stages, but the anticipation is building as Hudson and his team collaborate with Lucasfilm Games.
