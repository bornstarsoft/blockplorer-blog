The excitement is building as BeXide announces that the multiplayer party game, Fruit Mountain Party, will be making its debut on the Switch 2 and Switch in spring 2026. Currently available in Early Access on PC, this vibrant 3D puzzle game has captured the attention of gamers with its unique fruit-combining mechanics and engaging multiplayer modes.

Players will have the opportunity to experience the Switch version for the first time at upcoming gaming events, including Osaka Game Dungeon and Tokyo eSports Festa 2026. With features like heated online battles and a single-player mode that supports global rankings, Fruit Mountain Party promises to deliver a fun and competitive experience for players of all ages.
