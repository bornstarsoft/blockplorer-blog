The upcoming cozy diorama puzzle game, Woodo, is set to charm players on Nintendo Switch and Switch 2 this summer. Revealed during the Nintendo Indie World Showcase, the game invites players to assemble delightful wooden dioramas while following the adventures of Foxy and Ben the frog. With its adorable character designs and engaging gameplay, Woodo promises to be a must-play for cozy game enthusiasts.

In Woodo, players will place wooden objects into beautifully crafted 3D scenes, with the ability to reposition the camera for the perfect view. Each piece comes to life with animations, making the diorama-building experience dynamic and interactive. Notably, the Switch 2 version will utilize Joy-Con 2 mouse controls, enhancing the tactile satisfaction of gameplay. Additionally, a Steam demo is already available for PC players, allowing them to get a taste of the charming puzzles ahead of the full release.
