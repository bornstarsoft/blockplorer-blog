A new puzzle game titled "What's the Password?" is set to launch on May 28, 2026, and it promises to deliver a refreshing take on puzzle-solving. Developed by solo creator Dan DiIorio of TrampolineTales, this game emphasizes simplicity and engagement, featuring over 100 unique 4-digit password puzzles that challenge players without unnecessary distractions. The minimalist black and white graphics ensure that players can focus entirely on the puzzles at hand.

The game is designed for multiple platforms, including Android, iOS, and PC, making it accessible to a wide audience. With randomized solutions and optional hints for tougher challenges, players can expect a satisfying experience that encourages critical thinking. The inclusion of achievements adds an extra layer of motivation for those who love to showcase their skills.

As we look forward to the release, it's exciting to see how "What's the Password?" will resonate with puzzle enthusiasts. Will this minimalist approach redefine how we engage with mobile games? Let's find out soon!
