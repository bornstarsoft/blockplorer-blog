Toukana Interactive has just announced an exciting new chapter for their beloved puzzle game, Dorfromantik. The upcoming Medieval Biome Pack is set to transform the game's serene landscapes into a charming medieval world, complete with iconic tiles like a medieval trading cog and a mighty fortress. This cosmetic DLC not only enhances the visual appeal but also immerses players in a historical setting that promises to breathe new life into the gameplay experience.

In addition to the paid expansion, players can look forward to a free Anniversary Update that introduces integrated leaderboards and a long-requested Night Mode. The Night Mode will provide a cozy atmosphere with soft blue tones, perfect for evening gaming sessions. As Dorfromantik celebrates its 5th anniversary, these updates reflect the studio's commitment to enhancing player experience and community engagement.

With both the Medieval Biome Pack and the Anniversary Update scheduled for release in May/June 2026, fans are eagerly anticipating how these features will enrich their gameplay. What are you most excited to explore in this medieval transformation of Dorfromantik?
