On February 17, 2026, the BAFTA-nominated game Vampire Therapist will make its debut on PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, and Nintendo Switch, with Xbox One and Xbox Series X|S versions following on March 5. This game uniquely combines the elements of a narrative visual novel with a therapy simulation, allowing players to step into the shoes of a vampire therapist who helps immortal beings confront their traumas. The games engaging narrative, enhanced by a star-studded voice cast, promises a captivating experience that blends dark comedy with serious themes.

Developed by Little Bat Games, Vampire Therapist invites players to explore the lives of vampires from various historical eras, each with their own unique backstory and psychological challenges. With gameplay that incorporates elements of cognitive-behavioral therapy (CBT) and mini-games like mindfulness meditation, this title stands out in the gaming landscape. As players navigate the complexities of vampire existence, they will encounter a gothic atmosphere reminiscent of beloved works like What We Do in the Shadows.

As the gaming community eagerly anticipates the console release, one cant help but wonder: how will this innovative approach to storytelling and therapy resonate with players? Will Vampire Therapist redefine the boundaries of narrative-driven games?
