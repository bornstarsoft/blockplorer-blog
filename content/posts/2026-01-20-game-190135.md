Astro Loopers is revolutionizing the puzzle genre by merging classic Sokoban-style gameplay with innovative time loop mechanics and unique character skills. Players are challenged to navigate through over 150 stages, utilizing the power of their past selves to solve intricate puzzles and collect the elusive Fragments of Time scattered across the universe.

Each character in Astro Loopers possesses a distinct ability that interacts with blocks in various wayswhether breaking, pushing, or pulling them. This adds a layer of strategy to the gameplay, as mastering each character's skill is essential to overcoming the diverse and tricky mechanics presented in the game, such as slippery ice floors and explosive blocks.

With a free demo available that allows players to experience 24 stages, Astro Loopers invites puzzle enthusiasts to embark on a journey filled with logic and creativity. As you dive into this captivating adventure, how will you leverage time loops to conquer the challenges ahead?
