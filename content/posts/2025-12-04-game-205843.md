The latest update for Pokmon Friends, version 1.1.0, brings a host of exciting new features that puzzle enthusiasts and Pokmon fans alike will appreciate. With the addition of new puzzles, plushies, and furniture, players can now enjoy an enhanced gaming experience on their mobile devices and Nintendo Switch. The update also introduces new friend quests and increases the maximum friendship level, making it easier to connect with your favorite Pokmon.

One of the standout changes in this update is the increase in the maximum number of plushies you can place from 20 to 25, allowing for even more customization in your in-game space. Additionally, the introduction of tilt controls and adjustable puzzle difficulty levels ensures that players of all skill levels can find enjoyment and challenge in their gameplay. With these updates, Pokmon Friends continues to evolve, keeping its community engaged and entertained.
