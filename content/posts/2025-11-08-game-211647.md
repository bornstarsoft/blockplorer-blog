In a recent update, EA and BioWare have dispelled rumors regarding the status of the highly anticipated Mass Effect 5, reassuring fans that the game is indeed still in development. Amidst concerns stemming from EA's upcoming sale to private investors and the downsizing of BioWare's team, executive producer Mike Gamble emphasized the studio's commitment to expanding the Mass Effect universe and delivering more captivating stories.

Gamble's message came during the annual 'N7 Day' celebration, where he acknowledged the community's eagerness for news and confirmed that the development team is fully focused on the next installment. With a wealth of lore to explore and new gameplay features to implement, BioWare is dedicated to crafting an experience that will resonate with both long-time fans and newcomers alike.

As we await further updates, the excitement surrounding Mass Effect 5 continues to build. What new adventures and characters will we encounter in this beloved universe? Only time will tell, but one thing is clear: the journey is far from over.
