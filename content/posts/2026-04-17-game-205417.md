A Storied Life: Tabitha invites players to embark on a unique journey through the memories of a home, cleverly intertwining narrative and puzzle elements. Developed by Lab42, this game allows players to explore the life of Tabitha by packing up her belongings, each item revealing a piece of her story. This innovative approach to storytelling not only engages players but also encourages them to reflect on their own memories and the significance of the objects we hold dear.

Available now on Nintendo Switch and PC (Steam), A Storied Life: Tabitha offers a fresh perspective on how games can convey emotional depth through gameplay mechanics. As we dive into Tabitha's world, we are reminded of the power of memories and how they shape our identities. Will you take the time to unpack your own stories while playing this heartfelt game?
