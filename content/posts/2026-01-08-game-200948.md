The launch of AiliA, a captivating mirror-based puzzle game developed by IceToad Studio LLC, is making waves in the gaming community. Now available on Steam for $11.99, with a limited-time 20% discount, this game invites players to dive into a beautifully crafted world where reality and reflection intertwine. AiliA is not just another puzzle game; it challenges players to manipulate mirrors in a way that transforms their gameplay experience, making each puzzle a unique adventure.

Players step into the shoes of Aria, a young girl navigating through a series of visually stunning environments that span both the real and mirrored worlds. The game features over 100 puzzles, each designed to engage players with innovative mechanics involving water reflections and recursive mirror structures. AiliA also offers a free demo on Steam, allowing players to explore its enchanting world before committing to a purchase.

With a special collaboration bundle featuring Moncage, puzzle enthusiasts have even more reason to dive into this mesmerizing game. As we look forward to seeing how players interact with AiliA's unique mechanics, one can't help but wonder: will this game redefine the puzzle genre for a new generation?
