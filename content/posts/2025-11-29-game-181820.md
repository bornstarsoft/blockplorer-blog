Get ready for a magical adventure as ININ, in collaboration with Studio Siesta and Rocket Engine, gears up to release Trouble Witches FINAL! Episode 01 Daughters of Amalgam. Launching on December 15 for Nintendo Switch and PlayStation 4, this game brings back the beloved Cute 'Em Up genre with a fresh twist and a host of new features that will delight both old fans and newcomers alike.

The Physical Special Edition, available from December 9, offers collectors a treasure trove of goodies, including an original soundtrack and a full-color artbook. With 12 adorable witches to choose from, each with unique abilities and combat styles, players can dive into exhilarating bullet-hell gameplay that challenges even the most skilled gamers. This release not only revives a classic but also enhances it with stunning visuals and endless replayability. Are you ready to summon your magic and take on the challenge?
