In a world where gaming often demands intense focus and quick reflexes, Raccoon Cocoon stands out as a delightful escape. This cozy puzzle game offers a collection of 60 engaging puzzles that are perfect for those moments when you want to unwind yet still challenge your mind. With no time limits and the ability to replay puzzles, its designed for players who appreciate a leisurely gaming experience.

Developed by Kekik in the Bag, Raccoon Cocoon invites you into the charming world of Grandma Raccoon, where each puzzle is a step towards relaxation. Whether youre piecing together jigsaws or organizing tasks, the games soothing graphics and audio create a perfect backdrop for a cozy gaming session. If youve enjoyed titles like A Little to the Left or Tell Me Your Story, Raccoon Cocoon is sure to resonate with you.
