This December, PlayStation Plus is bringing a stellar lineup to its Game Catalog, featuring major titles like **Assassins Creed Mirage** and **Wo Long: Fallen Dynasty**. These additions are sure to excite gamers looking for new adventures and challenges. With the inclusion of **Skate Story** already available, players have plenty to dive into right now.

In addition to these blockbuster titles, the Classic Games Catalog welcomes the critically acclaimed **Soulcalibur 3**, exclusively for Premium members. This month also sees the arrival of family-friendly options like **Paw Patrol** games and **Lego Horizon Adventures**, ensuring there's something for everyone.
