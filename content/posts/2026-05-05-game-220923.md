The gaming community is buzzing with excitement as Glootschke Games and Coin Drop Games prepare to launch "The Remake of the End of the Greatest RPG of All Time" on May 28th. This unique deduction puzzle game invites players to delve into the climax of a fictional 90s JRPG, challenging them to uncover the secret behind the title's intriguing question: What is the Greatest RPG of All Time?

Set in a captivating 4-6 hour adventure, players will collect manual pages, watch clips from an unreleased documentary, and listen to director's commentary as they piece together the game's mysteries. With a blend of nostalgia and innovative gameplay, this title promises to blur the lines between fact and fiction, offering a truly meta experience that will resonate with both seasoned gamers and newcomers alike.

As we count down to the release, one can't help but wonder: will this game redefine our understanding of what makes an RPG great? Stay tuned for more updates as we approach launch day!
