Hungry Studio's flagship title, Block Blast!, has been recognized as the "Best Puzzle Game" at the Sensor Tower APAC Awards 2025, a testament to its popularity and player engagement across the globe. With over 70 million daily active users and ranking No. 1 in over 40 markets, Block Blast! has successfully captivated a diverse audience through its innovative gameplay mechanics and localized strategies tailored for the APAC region.

The game's unique "drag-match-disappear" experience, combined with its Classic and Adventure modes, has made it a favorite among players, blending accessibility with structured challenges. Hungry Studio's commitment to player-first strategies, including prefecture-based high-score challenges in Japan and celebrity collaborations in Korea, has further solidified its place in the hearts of gamers.

As we look ahead, the success of Block Blast! raises an intriguing question: how will Hungry Studio continue to innovate and adapt in the ever-evolving landscape of mobile gaming? The journey is just beginning, and we can't wait to see what they will achieve next.
