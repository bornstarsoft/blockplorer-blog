Nintendo has taken a significant step in enhancing its Nintendo Switch Online + Expansion Pack service by adding five new titles from the Virtual Boy's game library. This update brings the total number of available games to 12 in the West, with classics like V-Tetris and Jack Bros. now accessible to players. This move not only caters to nostalgia but also highlights Nintendo's commitment to preserving its gaming history.

With only 22 games originally released for the Virtual Boy, the addition of these titles means that players will soon have access to the majority of this unique library. While some games remain unconfirmed due to licensing issues, the anticipation for the remaining titles, including Mario Clash and unreleased gems like Zero Racer, keeps the excitement alive among fans. As Nintendo continues to roll out these classic games, one can't help but wonder: what other retro treasures might make their way to the Switch in the future?
