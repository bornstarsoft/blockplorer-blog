Pixel art enthusiasts and puzzle game fans alike have something to look forward to this June with the launch of Hazard Pay for PlayStation 5, Switch, and PC. Developed by Smitner Studio and published by Numskull Games, this game promises to deliver a unique blend of stealth, survival horror, and narrative choice, all wrapped in a beautifully crafted pixel art aesthetic.

Set in a Cold War-era research facility, players will step into the role of a government cleanup specialist tasked with navigating through a nightmarish environment filled with deadly bio-organisms. The games ingenious push/pull puzzle mechanics will challenge players to think critically while they uncover the dark secrets hidden within the facility. With branching narrative choices that impact the story's outcome, every decision could lead to a different fate for both the protagonist and the trapped scientists.

As we await the full release, the demo available now on PC offers a tantalizing glimpse into the horrors and puzzles that await. Will you be able to survive the chaos and expose the truth, or will you become just another expendable worker in this chilling narrative? The countdown to June begins!
