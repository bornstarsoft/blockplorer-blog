In a world where indie games often struggle for recognition, Fahim Faysal's journey with Compress(space) stands out as a beacon of creativity and resilience. This minimalist puzzle game, which challenges players to manipulate space itself, is not just a product of innovative design but also a testament to the determination of a Bangladeshi developer who refused to be deterred by the lack of local support.

Fahim's story is a compelling reminder of the potential that lies within underrepresented regions in the gaming industry. With a multinational team and the backing of the Draknek New Voices Puzzle Grant, Compress(space) is set to make waves on platforms like Steam and Xbox, showcasing the unique perspectives and talents emerging from Bangladesh. As we look to the future, one can't help but wonder: what other hidden gems are waiting to be discovered in the global gaming landscape?
